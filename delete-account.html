Delete Account – Livo

Account deletion information for Google Play users
In-app account deletion path

Open the Livo app and go to:
Settings → Account → Delete Account

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the Livo app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Follow the in-app steps to confirm account deletion.

If you cannot access the app, you can also contact support and request deletion:

Email: goodfortune.app.ss@gmail.com

What data will be deleted

  • Account link associated with your app account
  • Account-linked backup data stored under our control

What data may be retained

  • Anonymous aggregated analytics data that cannot reasonably identify an individual user
  • Data retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or operational reasons

Retention period

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable period, typically within 30 days.
