Livo Referral & Rewards Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-01

Livo offers a referral system that allows users to invite others and receive rewards when valid referrals are completed. This policy explains how referrals work, how rewards are granted, and what limitations and protections apply.

1. How referrals work

Each user may receive a unique referral code. This code can be shared with others. When a new user enters this code and completes the referral process, the referral is recorded.

Referral processing is account-based and is not tied to a specific device or platform. This means referrals may occur across Android and iOS devices.

2. Referral validation

Not all referral claims are automatically considered valid. Livo may apply validation checks to ensure that referrals are genuine and not the result of abuse or artificial activity.

A referral may remain in a pending state until validation is completed. Livo reserves the right to approve or reject any referral claim.

3. Rewards

When a referral is validated:

  • The invited user may receive a Premium reward.
  • The referring user may also receive a Premium reward.

Rewards may include Premium access for a limited duration. The exact duration may change over time and may be displayed inside the app.

When a user reaches a defined number of validated referrals (for example, 10), an extended reward such as a longer Premium period may be granted.

Extended rewards are applied starting from the moment the threshold is reached, not from earlier referral activity.

4. Referral limits

  • A user may only claim one referral code.
  • Self-referrals are not allowed.
  • Duplicate or repeated claims may be rejected.

5. Abuse prevention

Livo uses technical and behavioral signals to detect suspicious or abusive referral activity.

This may include patterns related to device usage, account behavior, rapid account creation, or other indicators of non-genuine referrals.

Livo may reject, revoke, or adjust rewards if abuse is detected.

6. Cross-platform usage

Referral codes are designed to work across platforms. A referral generated on Android may be used on iOS, and vice versa, as long as both users are authenticated accounts.

Account identity is determined by the authentication method used (such as Google or Apple), and separate sign-in methods may create separate accounts unless explicitly linked.

7. Reward adjustments

Livo reserves the right to:

  • Modify reward durations
  • Change referral thresholds
  • Limit or discontinue referral campaigns

Any such changes will apply to future referrals and may be reflected داخل the app interface.

8. Fair use

The referral system is intended for genuine user growth. Any attempt to exploit, automate, or manipulate the system may result in:

  • Referral rejection
  • Removal of rewards
  • Account restrictions

9. No monetary value

Referral rewards have no direct monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash or transferred between users.

10. Changes to this policy

Livo may update this policy from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date will be updated.
