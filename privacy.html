Livo Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-08

Livo is a personal finance app built to help users organize money activity while keeping core financial records primarily on the device. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed, how optional analytics work, how account-linked backup and restore operate, how anonymized and aggregated insight datasets may be generated, and what choices users have.

1. Core app data and local storage

Livo is designed so that day-to-day financial records such as transactions, categories, due reminders, notes, and similar entries are primarily stored on the user’s device.

This local-first design is intended to reduce unnecessary transmission of personal financial information and keep users in control of their records.

2. Account-linked backup and restore

If you sign in with Google or Apple and use account-linked restore features, Livo may store a server-side backup snapshot associated with your authenticated account so that your data can be restored when you change devices, reinstall the app, or recover access after data loss.

This backup snapshot is processed as service data used to provide app functionality. It is not treated as a commercial data product and is not sold as raw personal financial information.

3. Anonymous analytics

If anonymous analytics is enabled, Livo may collect limited non-identifying usage analytics to improve stability, reliability, feature quality, localization, and product development.

Examples may include app version, device type, screen size group, operating system family and version, general feature usage patterns, coarse regional information, and anonymized spending behavior signals such as category keys, amount buckets, payment method type, or time-of-use buckets.

Livo is intended not to include raw financial notes, exact transaction history, merchant raw text, direct personal identifiers, exact account balances, exact GPS coordinates, email addresses, phone numbers, or account identifiers in anonymous analytics events.

4. Aggregated data and analytics

Livo may generate anonymized and aggregated statistical datasets derived from user interaction patterns and financial behavior.

These datasets may be used for analytics, research, benchmarking, product improvement, and economic insight services, including delivery through data APIs.

No personal identifiers or account-linked financial records are included in such datasets.

All research datasets are aggregated and fully anonymized.

These datasets cannot be used to identify individual users.

Only aggregated statistical insights may be shared via research APIs.

5. Location insights

Livo may use approximate location information for regional statistics, localized financial trends, language and currency improvements, and similar localization features.

Only approximate location information such as city or region may be used. This data is not linked to user identity.

These settings are optional and can be changed later in Settings → Legal & Privacy → Data & Analytics.

6. Data sharing and commercial use

Livo does not sell personal financial records, raw transaction history, notes, exact balances, direct personal identifiers, or account-linked backup records.

Livo may use anonymous and aggregated analytics internally and may share, publish, or license anonymized, aggregated, non-identifying insight outputs for research, benchmarking, analytics, or product improvement purposes.

Any such use is intended to be based on transformed, aggregated, and non-identifying information rather than on a user’s raw personal financial records.

7. User controls

  • You can review the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service inside the app.
  • You can disable anonymous analytics and location-related settings later in Settings → Legal & Privacy → Data & Analytics.
  • You can request account data deletion using the support options provided in the app.

8. Data retention and deletion

Account-linked backup data is retained only as needed to provide restore and account-related functionality, subject to operational, security, and legal requirements.

When users delete an account or request deletion of account-linked data, Livo will make commercially reasonable efforts to remove account-linked backup data under its control, subject to applicable legal obligations and technical limitations.

Anonymous aggregated statistics that can no longer reasonably be linked back to an individual user may be retained.

9. Security

Livo is designed to minimize unnecessary external data transfer and to keep most user-entered financial information on-device.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so users are also responsible for securing access to their own devices and sign-in methods.

10. Children

Livo is not directed to children and is not intended for use by children below the minimum age required by applicable law in the user’s jurisdiction.

11. Policy changes

Livo may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When material changes are made, the effective date and in-app policy version may be updated accordingly.
